A Bit About Me...

My name is Matthew Williams II, I'm 15, and I am a Web Developer. I participated in the South Bend Code School's Fundementals of Web Development. In that, I created two websites and learned three coding/programming languages in nine weeks. After, I presented a web app with the group I was in to Google tech employees. Since then, I continue my work in web development not only by working on personal projects, but also working with the South Bend Code School as a Contract Developer and as a Freelance Developer. This is my passion, I would like to continue my work in Computer Science.

Right now I am a sophomore at Riley High School in the Engineering Magnet, continuing my education in computer science. In the future, I would like to find my self getting my degree in computer science from MIT and being a software engineer at Google.

While I was at Lasalle Intermediate Academy, I created a coding club in my eigth grade year where I taught web development and basic computer science. I did this to allow other kids to experince and get exposed to a big and upcoming industry.
